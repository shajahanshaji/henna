Our Story

Nafee Organic Henna was born from a passion for natural beauty and intricate mehndi artistry. We believe that every bride deserves pure, chemical-free henna that leaves a rich, deep stain without harming the skin. Our cones are handmade with organic ingredients, sourced sustainably, and crafted with love. Our artists blend traditional motifs with contemporary elegance to create bespoke designs for weddings and celebrations.
